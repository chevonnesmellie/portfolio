about me.

front-end developer based in Mississauga, ON

After I earned by Bachelor of Science in Biology & Health Studies at the University of Toronto, I enrolled in a year-long course in Bioinformatics at Seneca College. This is where I was first introduced to the endless capabilities of coding and computer science. I found it to be facinating and very enjoyable, however upon completion of that course, my career took off in a different direction, and I regretfully stopped coding. In 2021, when I was looking for new challenges and opportunities professionally, I decided to return to coding and development, where I know there is always more to learn and room to grow.
